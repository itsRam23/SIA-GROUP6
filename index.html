About Basketball

Basketball is a fast-paced sport played between two teams of five players each. The objective is to score by shooting the ball through the opponent's hoop. It is one of the most popular sports globally, with millions of fans worldwide.

Whether you're playing, watching, or learning about the sport, there's always something exciting happening in the world of basketball!
